# ASTRA Surfaces

## Incident-state surface

The hidden security state of hosts, services, or attack stages.

## Telemetry surface

Events, logs, alerts, and observations emitted by monitoring sources.

## Telemetry impairment surface

Delay, loss, noise, duplication, overload, corruption, and adversarial suppression.

## Defender-belief surface

The maintained estimate of host compromise, attack stage, or incident state.

## Triage/action surface

Analyst effort, alert prioritization, host inspection, and query budget.

## Usefulness-diagnostics surface

Delivery, staleness, redundancy, misleadingness, entropy, belief error, and effort-aware usefulness.
